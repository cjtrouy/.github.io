  • About Us

    Welcome to Shutter Alaska! My name is Ethan and I am the sole photographer behind this one-man show. I specialize in capturing the wild beauty of Alaska through my lens, and I'm here to offer you a range of photography services that will help you capture your own piece of Alaska's stunning landscapes.

    At Shutter Alaska, I offer a range of photography services that are perfect for anyone who wants to capture their own unique Alaskan experience. From family portraits to adventure photography, I've got you covered. I offer on-location photo shoots throughout Alaska, whether it's in the mountains, by the ocean, or anywhere in between.

    My goal is to capture the essence of Alaska in every photo I take. Whether you're looking for a beautiful landscape shot or a candid family portrait, I'll work with you to create images that you'll cherish for a lifetime. I believe that photography is all about telling a story, and I'm passionate about capturing the stories that make Alaska so special.

    So if you're looking for a photographer who is passionate about Alaska and dedicated to capturing the beauty of the Last Frontier, look no further than Shutter Alaska. Contact me today to learn more about my services and to schedule your own photo shoot. I can't wait to work with you!
